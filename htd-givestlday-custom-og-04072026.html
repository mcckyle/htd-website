Highway To Destiny will launch a new program (Job Links) which is TBA. The program will fund vocational training for the unhoused in St. Louis, with the goal of self-sufficiency. Students will be trained in Truck Driving, Automotive Services, Inspections, Welding, HVAC, Robotics and other fields. This training will prepare students to fill vacancies in the vocational trades market. Their certifications will open doors for new opportunities that can lead to financial stability.
