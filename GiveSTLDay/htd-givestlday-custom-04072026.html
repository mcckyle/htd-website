Job Links Program

A pathway to skills, stability, and self-sufficiency for individuals ready to rebuild their future.

Highway To Destiny is launching Job Links, a workforce development program designed to fund vocational training for unhoused individuals in Saint Louis.

Participants will gain certifications in high-demand fields:

Truck Driving • Automotive Services • Welding • HVAC • Inspections • Robotics

These skills open doors to stable employment and financial independence.

Your support turns opportunity into transformation.
