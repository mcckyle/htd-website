In 2024, 23 of every 10,000 people in the U.S. experienced homelessness. Families with children saw the largest single-year increase — 39% higher than 2023. Nearly 150,000 children experienced homelessness on a single night in 2024 — a 33% increase from 2023. In 2024, about 1 in 5 people experiencing homelessness were age 55+. 46% of adults 55+ were unsheltered in places not meant for human habitation. Saint Louis County’s 2025 PIT count: 389 unhoused persons. City of St. Louis’ 2025 PIT count: 1,637 unhoused persons. In 2024, 23 of every 10,000 people in the U.S. experienced homelessness.
